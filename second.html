    Like most people, my first consoles were from Nintendo. The Nintendo DS was my first video game console, and it introduced me to games that would help me formulate my preferences for games. Some of the more notable games were Star Wars Battlefront: Elite Squadrons, Mario Kart DS, New Super Mario Bros., & Lego Star Wars: The Complete Saga. My first memorable moments with video games come from some of these games. For example, I will never forget being stuck on World 8-8 for months when I was trying to beat New Super Mario Bros. for my sister. Soon after the DS came the Wii, which only allowed me to explore more titles, like Wii Sports & Wii Sports Resort, while also learning that video games could also be fun when playing with friends and family. Storytelling in games became more meaningful to me with titles such as Super Mario Galaxy 2, Star Wars: The Clone Wars - Republic Heroes, Rabbids Go Home, & Wall-E. These consoles showed me how different games could offer a fun experience, but, most importantly, they left me wanting a larger and more grandeur digital experience.
